It's Time to Go Camping

This conference is being held to allow developers to come and share their experiences and to learn from each other. Code Camp is about challenging the status-quo, continually looking for ways to better our craft, and to collaborate to create a community of developers. This conference is FREE, though registration is limited, so register today!
